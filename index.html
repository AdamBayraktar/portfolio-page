WHAT I DO

Building websites from scratch

As a web developer I create websites that meet my clients' needs. I can either build a website from scratch using programming languages, or utilize popular content management systems (CMS) to develop the site. Regardless of the approach, my goal is to ensure that the website is functional, visually appealing, and meets the specific requirements of the client.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

I offer SEO services for my web pages. This involves optimizing the website's content and structure to improve its visibility and ranking on search engines like Google. By using keyword research, link building, and other techniques, I aim to increase the website's organic traffic and improve its relevance to search engines.

Maintaining and updating websites

Once a website is launched, I am responsible for maintaining and updating it as needed. This includes making sure that the site remains secure and up-to-date, adding new features and functionality, and ensuring that the site continues to meet the client's needs.
